MISSION AND VISION OF SPORTS PSYCHOLOGY ASSOCIATION OF INDIA

The Sports Psychology Association of India (SPAI), like most professional organizations, likely has a mission and vision aimed at promoting the field of sports psychology and supporting the mental well-being and performance of athletes.

Mission

The mission of the Sports Psychology Association of India (SPAI) is to:
  1. Promote the field of Sports Psychology by fostering research, education and professional practice in Sports Psychology across India.
  2. Enhance athlete performance and well-being by integrating psychological principles and techniques into sports training, coaching, and management.
  3. Develop professional standards and provide certification, training and continuous education for sports psychologists, coaches, and other stakeholders in the sports ecosystem.
  4. Collaborate with sports organizations to create mental training programs that improve focus, resilience, motivation, and emotional control in athletes.
  5. Research and development, promoting research in the field of sports psychology and advancing scientific knowledge to benefit athletes and sports organization.

Vision

The Vision of Sports psychology Association of India is to:
  1. Be the leading authority in sports psychology in India, advancing the mental and emotional well-being of athletes and contributing to their holistic development.
  2. Create a culture where sports psychology is recognized as essential to athletic success and is seamlessly integrated into all levels of sports training and competition.
  3. Empower athletes to achieve their highest potential through scientifically supported psychological interventions, ensuring balanced performance and well-being.
  4. Foster excellence in the field of sports psychology by developing highly skilled professionals who contribute to national and international sporting success.
  5. Promote mental health and resilience in sports by increasing the awareness and application of sports psychology in abundant sports.
  6. Building professional networks, creating a strong network of sports psychologist across India and fostering collaboration with international bodies to share best practices.
