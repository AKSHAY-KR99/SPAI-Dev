Dr. Anil Karwande

President of SPAI

Dr Anil Karwande is President of Sports Psychology Association of India. Dr Anil has done his PhD in Physical Education. He’s the founder of NET JRF GURUKUL. He’s the academic counselor for Solapur University and the Vice-President Vishwa Punarnirman Sangh. He is the Former Principal of IDCPE Nagpur and a former member board of Management LNIPE Deemed University Gwalior. He's working on a project to Increase awareness of entry into the Defense Forces. He is the President of KREEDA BHARTI Vidarbha-Prant and Maharashtra. He has received a letter of Recognition by Indian Association of Sports Medicine for the upliftment of Sports Medicine in the country.He has received Rajiv Gandhi Education Excellence Award given by the Indian Society Council. He has received Bharat Vidya Ratan Award 2010 given by the Health and Development Association. He has received the Ideal Teacher Award 2013 given by Maharastra State Physical & Educational Teacher Association. He has participated in more than 100 seminars and conference at national and international level.

Prof. (Dr.) M.L. Kamlesh

Patron of SPAI

Decorated with the accolade of Doctor of Science by the Open International University of Medicina Alternativa, Colombo (Sri Lanka) for his outstanding contribution to the science of physical education, Professor M.L. Kamlesh (b.1940), M.A. (English), M.Ed. (Physical Education), Ph.D., well-known for excellence in academics, research, sport psychology and administration in India and abroad, occupies the top-spot among the fraternity of physical educationists in the country. Dr. Kamlesh has authored 29 books and published more than 72 research papers and 62 articles in national and international journals and periodicals and given 77 keynote addresses and invited lectures at various national (181) and international conferences (30).This former Principal, of the Sports Authority of India’s Laxmibai National College of Physical Education, Thiruvananthapuram, and former Dean, Faculty of Physical Education, University of Kerala, with an outstanding career of more than 45 years, was the founder member of Sports Psychology Association of India, which he very successfully served first as Secretary General for two terms, and then as President for next two terms. He has been bestowed upon Life-time Achievement Award by(1) Sports Psychology Association of India in 2004 for distinguished role in the development of sport psychology in the country; (2) the Alumni Association of Physical Education, Jiwaji University, Gwalior in 2005; (3) University of Delhi (2011); (4) by Punjabi University, Patiala (2012); and by (5) Guru scientist, University, Amritsar (2014) for rendering outstanding service to physical education and sport as a teacher, a researcher, a scientist and an administrator. He was awarded ASPASP Fellowshipat the 8th International Congress of Asian-South Pacific Association of Sport Psychology, June 29-July 3, 2018, organized by the Korean Society of Sport Psychology at Keimyung University, Deagu, Republic Korea. SPAI Presidential Award – 2023 for Excellence in Promoting Sports Psychology. Awarded at Dr. Ambedkar College, Deekasha Bhoomi, Nagpur on 28 March 2023 at the closing of the 30th National Conference of Sports Psychology.

Dr. Anuradha. S. Solanky

Dr. Anuradha. S. Solanky completed her M.Phil&Ph.D in Psychology from Bangalore University in the year 1989-1998. Currently employed with “Sports Authority of India” as Sports Psychologist, since July 1993.Job profile includes Research, Providing Psychological support to Indian National teams, Counseling, Teaching (Diploma & Refresher Course), mental training, psychological testing & Assessment. Trained under Russian Psychologist, Attended Sports Science training in University of Birmingham & Certification of “Mind room training” by Dr. Pierre Beauchamp, Canada. Member of Academic council, paper setter & Examiner for many Universities. Life member of Sports Psychology Association of India (SPAI) and Indian Association of Sports Medicine (IASM) also member of International Society of Sports Psychology (ISSP). Attended Sports Science course at University of Birmingham (UK) in the year 2016. Attended many National & International conferences, Seminars, workshops. Presented research papers, conducted Seminars & educational programs for Coaches, Athletes, Physical Educationists etc.

Dr Jolly Roy

Dr Jolly Roy is a Professor of sport psychology at the Faculty of Sports and Exercise Sciences in the Centre for Sport Science at Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research (Deemed University), Chennai, India. She is also an ISSP registered sport psychology practitioner. Previously Jolly Roy was a faculty engaged in education and research at University of Science Malaysia; She served as a senior consultant (sport psychology) at the National Sports Institute of Malaysia and as a sports scientist at Sports Authority of India. She also served as a Professor in Sport Psychology at Manav Rachna University,Faridabad,Delhi,India. Dr Jolly has a PhD ,Masters degree in Psychology and MSW degree (psychiatry). She was trained in sport psychology at the Research Institute for Olympic Sports, Finland and at Florida State University, USA. Dr Jolly was engaged with the psychological preparation of the London Olympic Archery team of Malaysia, served as the psychologist for the Indian contingent for the Asian Games Busan, served as a psychologist for the Malaysian contingent for the Equestrian Endurance World Championships in Malaysia, and the South East Asian games in Laos, Indonesia, and Singapore. She has to her credit book, book chapters and research papers in peer-reviewed journals. Currently Dr Jolly is an elected managing council member of ASPASP. Previously she served as a managing council member of the International Society of Sport Psychology(ISSP) 2017-2021.
