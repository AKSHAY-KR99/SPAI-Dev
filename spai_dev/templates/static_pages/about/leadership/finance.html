FINANCE SECRETARY OF SPORTS PSYCHOLOGY ASSOCIATION OF INDIA

Prof (Dr.) Mahendran Kumar Singh

Treasurer of SPAI

Prof. (Dr.) M K Singh is presently working in the department of sport psychology at LNIPE, Gwalior.He is also a chief proctor of LNIPE & a Finance Secretary of SPAI. He has more than 30 Years teaching experience out which more than 17 years at Master's level. He has the following administrative experiences: Former Incharge Registrar, Former Dean Academics, Former Dean Sport Sciences, Former Dean Physical Education and Applied Areas & Former HOD, Department of Sport Psychology of LNIPE,Gwalior. He was also a Former HOD of Department of LAW at Guru Ghasidas Central University, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh and a Former HOD Dept. of Physical Education, CSJM University, Kanpur. He has Authored Five Books & Published More than 50 Research Papers in International/National Journals out of which 06 Papers are in SCOPUS Journals. He has also presented more than 35 papers at International/National Level Conference/Seminars.
