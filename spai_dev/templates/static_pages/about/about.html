ABOUT SPORTS PSYCHOLOGY ASSOCIATION OF INDIA

Sports Psychology Association of India is a growing platform that focuses on understanding how psychological factors affect performance and how participation in sports and exercise impacts psychological and physical well-being. As Indian athletes continue to make their mark in international sports, the demand for sports psychology services has grown. Over the last two decades, there has been increasing recognition of the role of mental training in achieving elite athletic performance, with a growing number of Indian athletes working with sports psychologist. Sports psychologist helps athletes to deal with pressures, anxiety related to competition, intensifies their concentration and manages stress. The Sports Psychology Association of India (SPAI) plays a key role in promoting sports psychology across India. The association organizes conferences, workshops, and training programs for professional and athletes. With Indian sports moving towards more scientific and data driven approaches, sports psychology is expected to grow significantly.
