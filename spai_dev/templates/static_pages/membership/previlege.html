SPAI MEMBERSHIP PRIVILEGES

PRIVILEGES

The Sports psychology Association of India (SPAI) offers numerous privileges to its members, which are designed to enhance the growth of sports psychology and provide critical support for athletes and professionals in the field. Membership privileges are:

Professional Networking

SPAI provides opportunities for members to connect with national and international professionals in sports psychology, through affiliations with organizations like the International Society of Sports Psychology and Asian South-Pacific Association of Sports psychology.

Research Access

SPAI offers members access to the latest research in sports psychology, including journals and publications. Members also get the opportunity to publish their own work in SPAI journal, contributing to the academic body of sports psychology knowledge in India.

Training and Education

Sports Psychology actively promote sports psychology education in India by supporting the creation of certified sports psychologists. Members can benefit from these initiatives by gaining access to specialized training and certification courses.

Awards and Recognition

SPAI acknowledges the contributions of its members through various awards such as the Best Research paper, Lifetime Achievement Award, and Scientist Award

These privileges position Sports Psychology Association of India to significantly contribute to the growth and development of sports psychology in India, while advancing their own careers through research, education and networking opportunities.
